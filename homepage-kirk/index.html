THE RESEARCHER

Human behavior is not noise. It is the signal.

My work studies how intelligent agents can perceive dynamic people and spaces, learn from human feedback, and act in a way that is useful rather than merely automated.

That shows up in 3D perception, multimodal fusion, interactive datasets, embodied learning, and human-robot collaboration.
