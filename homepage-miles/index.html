ABOUT

Researching human-aware intelligence in the physical world.

I am a Ph.D. student at ShanghaiTech University, advised by Prof. Yuexin Ma. My work combines LiDAR-camera fusion, human pose and motion understanding, interactive systems, and robot learning.

The throughline is simple: make intelligent agents better at perceiving people, learning from them, and collaborating with them in dynamic 3D spaces.
